Workshop Programs

Are you passionate about health messaging and disease classification? We invite professionals and students alike to be a part of our upcoming workshop by submitting abstracts for presentations. This is your chance to share your unique insights, groundbreaking research findings, or compelling case studies with a diverse audience of healthcare and technology professionals.

Submission Guidelines

  • Word Limit: Abstracts should not exceed 300 words, ensuring clarity and conciseness.
  • Title: Provide a clear and captivating title for your presentation that encapsulates your work's essence.
  • Structure: Your abstract should include sections highlighting the objectives, methods, results, and conclusions of your research or case study.
  • Relevance: Clearly articulate how your presentation aligns with the central theme of the workshop, emphasizing the significance of your work.
  • Contact Information: Include your full name, affiliation, contact details, and a brief biography highlighting your expertise in the subject matter.
  • Social Media Shoutout: Once submitted, remembder to give us a social media shoutout detailing the event and what you will be showcasing.
